Our history

The Oceanic Schools are a product of their founders who dared to dream, back in 1991. This was a turbulent year, when the country was faced with the prospects of "new politics." Despite the uncertainty of the new politics, they believed that a new school would serve the country favourably. Thus the founders Dr. Peter Keiyoro and his loving wife Tr. Esther Njenga founded the school in Kikuyu, Kenya, among some of the country's best and oldest educational institutions.

About

Mission

To nurture wholesome students.

Vision

Our vision is for each child to develop a curiosity of learning, discover their interests and grow in their love of learning. We also desire to have strong families through parent support/fellowship and skills training.

CORE VALUES

    EXCELLENCE – We will have only one standard excelence
    WELLNESS – We will strive to nurture mentally, physically, mentally and spiritually strong members of society.
    DETERMINATION – We encourage teamwork and strive to be the best in all our students do.
